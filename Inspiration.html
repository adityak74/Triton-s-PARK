INSPIRATION

In 2012, the US has half of the world's passenger cars (about 300 million units). The need for parking space will grow as the US population grows (projected to be 460 million people in 2050).It is obvious that people and their cars grow much faster than the expansion of parking spaces in cities. More than just convenience, lack of efficient parking can slow the economy down.

Triton's Park is a one-of-a-kind parking system aiming at:

  • Autonomous precision tracking/billing
  • Flexible and scalable system
  • Dynamic business models allowing advertising
  • Social networking enabled
